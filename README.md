# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing retail sales performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments.

The dashboard analyzes **9.8K retail orders generating $2.26M in revenue**, providing insights into regional performance, product demand, and customer purchasing behavior.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of overall sales performance including:

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Sales trends over time
- Regional sales comparison
- Category sales distribution
- Top performing products

### Product & Regional Analysis
This page provides deeper analytical insights into product performance and customer segments.

Key analysis includes:

- Sales by product sub-category
- Category performance by customer segment
- Top performing products
- Segment contribution to category revenue

<img src="Images/product_analysis.png" width="900">

---

## Key Insights

- The **West region** generates the highest revenue (~$0.71M).
- **Technology** is the highest-performing product category.
- The **Consumer segment** contributes nearly **50% of total revenue**.
- **Phones and Chairs** are the top-performing product subcategories.
